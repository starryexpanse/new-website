About us

Who We Are

We are a group of game developers who share the dream of seeing Riven released into the modern gaming landscape. The Starry Expanse Project is our attempt to immortalize Cyanâ€™s masterpiece and let players gaze in awe and wonder at the beauty of Riven's tropical and deserted landscapes, at the same time puzzling as to what exactly they have been brought there to do, just as players did worldwide, upon Riven's original release in 1997.

Unique Challenges

Beyond the usual wide array of work that goes into game development, we face a few interesting additional challenges:

Making Geometry Match 2D Images
Our game aims to have 1-to-1 parity with the original screens seen in Riven.
Getting a 3D scene to line up with a series of 2D images, then, is our main task, and this calls into existence our laborious-but-rewarding process of camera matching. We try not to let it take over our lives as much as it wishes it could.
All-Original Sculpting and Modeled Geometry
All our geometry is hand-crafted from scratch by our incredibly talented and hard-working modelers.
Check out the gallery for more!
Hugely Detailed Environments
Every bolt and lever is modeled in detail.
Believability is key.
Lifelike, Convincing Characters
Masterful performances by John Keston and Sheila Goold have set the bar high, and this calls for meticulous loyalty to detail and craftsmanship in our character models and animation.
Modernizing an Old Classic
No longer held back my the technical limitations of the original, we aspire to not only enhance the visuals but also improve or reiterate on aesthetics that may not have been accurate or logical in the original. Staying as faithful as we can to the original classic, while adding our own personal touch.
